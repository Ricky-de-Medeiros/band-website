Ricky developed his skills by learning from great masters of Classical and Traditional music from his homeland, Brazil. Ricky describes his style as “… a combination of Bossa Nova harmony with Classical guitar fingerstyle, a good dose of Latin grooves, lots of passion complemented by fiery flamenco techniques”. “What a talent, very authentic Latin style, a proper talent I would say… A natural who has worked very hard at his art”, said Keith Airey, session guitarist for Paul McCartney and Tom Jones, about Ricky.

In New Zealand, Ricky quickly impressed audiences with his fingerstyle guitar technique, and he went from busking on the streets of Auckland to performing at special events and venues across the contry.

Recognized as a reference in Latin guitar, Ricky was invited to record for NZ Music Award Winners LatinAotearoa.

He has performed live on TVNZ with the Sony Music NZ/Russian singer Yulia. As an accompanist, Ricky has performed with the Latin Grammy Award Winner Romulo Castro.

In 2018 he accompanied acclaimed Brazilian singer Alda Rezende headlining the annual Brazilian Day festival in Auckland. In 2019, Ricky was invited to play Brazil’s national anthem for the same festival with Samba Drum Ensemble Unidos de Aotearoa.

On the same year, Ricky joined forces with Jimy Kara (A.K.A Kara Gordon), a graduate from the prestigious Berkley College of Music. Kara opened for Tommy Emmanuel, Ozzy Osbourne, Slash and Elton John, played with Joe Satriani and Deep Purple. Ricky and Kara formed the guitar duo AcoustiCartel, currently Recording their first album. In 2020 the duo opened for the renowned Brazilian international Gabriel Sater. On that concert, they featured as special guests in one of Gabriel’s tunes.

Ricky is a prolific musician, constantly honing his skills. In Auckland, he has been performing multiple times a week at residencies and in high profile events for over a decade. Currently, he has a few studio recording projects underway, including his own instrumental album. Ricky also teaches music privately and in a few schools across the city. A passionate full-time musician in demand.
